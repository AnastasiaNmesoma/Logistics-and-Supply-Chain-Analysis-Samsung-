## Project Overview
Designed and developed an end-to-end Power BI dashboard to analyze Samsung’s supply chain performance across procurement, inventory, logistics, and customer operations.

The project transforms raw transactional data into actionable insights to support operational efficiency, cost optimization, and decision-making.

The analysis focuses on:
- Supply chain flow
- Inventory management
- Procurement and production efficiency
- Customer performance
- Sales and shipment performance

> All visualizations, data modeling, and analysis were built from scratch in Power BI.

> ## Important Note:
> For full context, including project objectives, & dataset description please refer to the [Project documentation](Samsung%20Logistics%20and%20Supply%20Chain%20analysis.md)

## Business Objectives
- Evaluate **supplier performance** (cost, lead time, quality)
- Monitor **inventory efficiency** and stock optimization
- Analyze **shipment reliability** and delay drivers
- Identify **high-value customers** and profitability trends

## Data & Modeling
A star schema was implemented with five fact tables.
- **Product** and **Date** act as shared dimensions across all fact tables
- **Customer** links to **Sales** and **Shipment**
- **Facility** connects to **Inventory**, **Sales**, and **Shipment**
- **Supplier** links exclusively to **Procurement**

## Key Metrics
- Revenue, Profit, Profit Margin
- Inventory Turnover & Days in Inventory
- Delivery % and Shipment Delays
- Supplier Lead Time & Quality Score
- Perfect Order Rate

## Dashboard Features
### Executive Overview
- High-level KPIs for supply chain performance
- Revenue contribution by customer
- Shipment delays and inventory distribution
### Supply (Procurement)
- Supplier comparison (lead time, cost, quality)
- Dynamic analysis (Unit Cost vs Order Quantity)
- Regional supplier performance
### Inventory & Production
- Inventory turnover and stock efficiency
- Safety stock vs current inventory
- Production defect analysis and trends
### Shipment (Logistics)
- Delivery performance and delay analysis
- Shipment cost trends
- Carrier performance comparison
### Customer Analytics
- Revenue and profit analysis (dynamic views)
- Customer contribution and segmentation
- Discount impact on sales and profitability

## Key Insights
Identified **supplier inefficiencies** through lead time and cost variation
Detected **slow-moving inventory** using turnover and inventory days
Highlighted **logistics bottlenecks** via delay and delivery metrics
Revealed **high-value customers** driving revenue and profit
